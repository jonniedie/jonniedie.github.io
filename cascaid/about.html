The Problem

Funding for open source software is a bit of a mess. Typically, funds (when there even are any) go only to those write user-facing software. Maintainers of projects further down in the dependency chain are unlikely to see any of that.

A Solution

With cascaid, project maintainers are able to mark portions of their incoming donations to flow down to projects in their dependency chain.

Support

cascaid does not collect any fees for this service. It does not run any ads. It does not track your behavior. It does not sell any of your data. If you would like cascaid to continue existing and can afford to support it, donations are greatly appreciated.
